About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Steve, I'm a beginner programmer, I'm learning on my own. Sometimes I have to work with images and change their format, I used different programs for this, but at some point I realized that I could make them a little better, then I decided to make my website for these purposes.

The project will be completely free, and I plan to provide payment for the server through advertising on the site or support for friendly site visitors.

While this is the first version of the site, the design is a bit clumsy, but in the future I will finalize it as well. I hope you enjoy using my site.

For development I used JavaScript 11ty, Svelte, Golang, Fiber
